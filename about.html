About Us

We are an organisation that aims at preserving the health and habitat of seals in Victoria, Australia. Our team is bent on clearing the plastics from the habitats of seals and e would like to see them grow into their deserving habitats. We, as a team are striving towards this goal, in the best interests of the seals.

Mission

Problems for these wonderful creatures are our fault and not theirs. Our mission is to improve the cleanliness of seals' and sea lions' habitats through rubbish collecting, rubbish log building and injured wildlife reporting.

It is a fact that living with danger of any form is unacceptable. We all need to come together in creating the environment that the marine animals deserve. We would like the marine habitat restored with humans' help. Intervening in their activities destroying their home is what has happened for a very long time. Now it is time to intervene for their lives to be given back to them. Through this initiative, we hope to achieve the best outcome for marine animals - let them live their lives without trouble from us. They also have equal rights on this planet as us.

Why work with us?

We want the seals along with other marine animals to have their habitats clean so that they can live peacefully. You can also have the experience of help save a seal through this initiative. Your service is important in restoring their livelihood back. Small droplets of water make a huge flood. So, why not join the cause and be a droplet?
