What is the mission?

Give their habitat back to them free from pollution.

What is it we aim to achieve.

It is certain that living with danger of any form is unacceptable. We all need to come together in creating the environment that the marine animals deserve. We would like the marine habitat restored with humans' help. Intervening in their activities destroying their home is what has happened for a very long time. Now it is time to intervene for their lives to be given back to them. Through this initiative, we hope to achieve the best outcome for marine animals - let them live their lives without trouble from us. They also have equal rights on this planet as us.
