Saving our animals while they are alive

Seals

Seals are playful creatures which are predominant in Victoria. There are mainly three kinds of seals and a kind of sea lion living around Victorian coastal line based on the collected data. They are Antarctic Fur-seal, Australian fur-seal, New Zealand Fur-seal and Australian Sea Lion. Antarctic Fur-seal and Australian Sea Lion are listed under the EPBC Act as Vulnerable.

Learn more

Problem

Seals are getting affected by the plastics in the ocean and beaches. They get entangled or ingest the plastics thinking it to be food. Some species even are endangered. Their playful nature is being exploited and their health damaged at a young age. This is a big concern as it our fault they are in this state.

Learn more

Plan

We can help the seals by removing the plastics from the beaches and oceans through volunteering activities and giving them their right to live peacefully.
